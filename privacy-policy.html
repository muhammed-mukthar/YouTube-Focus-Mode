üîê Permissions Explained

The extension requests minimal permissions, each with a specific purpose:

  • activeTab: Required to apply blur styles to YouTube pages you're viewing
  • storage: Required to save your on/off preference locally
  • host_permissions (youtube.com): Required to run the extension on YouTube website

We request only the permissions absolutely necessary for the extension to function. No more, no less.
